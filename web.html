THE WORKSHOP EXPERIENCE

Mastering Grain & Code

At ABC Wood Works, we exist at the intersection of natural beauty and digital precision. While we respect and preserve ancient carpentry techniques, we understand that modern design demands unrivaled accuracy and scalability.

Our workshop handles single custom architectural commissions for luxury residences, as well as high-volume batch processing of furniture parts, carved wall panels, and bespoke solid interior/exterior frames.

DIGITAL WOOD CARVING

Multi-Axis CNC Machining & Complex Routing

We house state-of-the-art flatbed CNC routers capable of multi-tool nested carving. This allows us to repeat incredibly intricate 3D designs, engrave corporate branding signage, and carve complex architectural relief patterns across vast dimensions.

Our workflow parses DXF, DWG, STEP, or high-fidelity 3D modeling files (.OBJ / STL) directly into specialized toolpaths, ensuring the physical cut matches the computer draft within a hair's breadth.
